Tatertot Casserole

Description

A meal for lovers of the greatest evolution of the potato since the potato chip, the tatertot. The cheesy, meaty, and potato filled Tatertot Casserole is perfect and quick to prep and cook. With all the cooking experience in the world, or none at all this dish will work for you and your family.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pinch salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 (16 oz) package frozen tater tots

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until no longer pink and completely browned, 7 to 10 minutes; season with salt and black pepper. Stir cream of mushroom soup into the cooked ground beef; pour the mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer tater tots evenly over the ground beef mixture; top with Cheddar cheese.
  3. Bake until tater tots are golden brown and hot, 30 to 45 minutes.
