Tatertot Casserole

Description

A meal for lovers of the greatest evolution of the potato since the potato chip, the tatertot. The cheesy, meaty, and potato filledTatertot Casserole is perfect and quick to prep and cook. With all the cooking experience in the world, or none at all this dish will work for you and your family.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pinch salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 (10.75 oz) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 (16 oz) package frozen tater tots
