Cheesy Skillet Lasagna

Description

The Easy Cheesy Skillet Lasagna, well like that name suggest, is very easy to prep and cook. The recipe contains, sausauge, mushrooms, and cheese on top or more cheesy! Prep time for the dish is a breeze with a 10 min prep time and 35 mins during the cooking process. Giving a snappy period of 45 minutes to feed the entire family. The meal contains 6 servings so it will do the family well and fill them right up.
