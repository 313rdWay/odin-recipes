Buttermilk Coleslaw

Description

One of the quickest and easiest sides that you could possible prep and prepare. Buttermilk Coleslaw 10 mins to prepare and just needs to sit for 40 minutes to be ready to eat. One of my favorite sides with any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mayonnaise
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 3 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon celery seed
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 (16 oz) packages shredded coleslaw mix

Steps

  1. n a large bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, buttermilk, sugar, celery seed and black pepper. Fold in the coleslaw mix, and refrigerate until serving. This tastes best if it is refrigerated for a few hours before serving.
