Slow Cooker Texas Pulled Pork

Description

This recipe requires the most patients out of all that we have covered so far. The Slow Cooker Texas Pulled Pork take a bit over 5 hour to fully prepare and cook. This is a delicious option for your family and persoanl one of my favorites.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vegtable oil
  • 1 (4lbs) pork shoulder roast
  • 1 cup barbeque sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 extra large onion, chopped
  • 2 large cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 8 hamburger bun, split
  • 2 tablesppons butter, or as needed
